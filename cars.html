Car Dealership Database Design


The purpose of this database is to manage and track the vehicle inventory, sales, and services at a Honda dealership. The system is designed to reflect realistic dealership operations, including inventory management, multiple sales of the same vehicle (e.g., trade-ins and resales), and service records. It supports tracking customer purchases, staff performance, and vehicle history.

Entity Definitions

Vehicle

Each car is identified by its VIN and may be sold and serviced multiple times throughout its lifetime.

Inventory

Tracks vehicles currently in stock. A unique Stock Number is used for each instance a vehicle enters the lot. If a vehicle is resold or returned, it receives a new Stock Number.

Customer

Individuals who purchase vehicles from the dealership.

Salesperson

Employees responsible for completing vehicle sales.

Sale

Each sale transaction includes one customer, one salesperson, and may involve one or more vehicles.

SoldVehicle

A junction table that connects each sale to the vehicles involved. This allows the same vehicle (VIN) to be sold multiple times and tracks which Stock Number was sold in each transaction.

Service

Maintains a record of service events (maintenance or repair) tied to a vehicle VIN, allowing for a full history of service regardless of ownership.

Conceptual Design and ERD


Entity Description
Vehicle A strong entity; attributes: VIN (Primary Key), Make, Model, Year, Price, Color, Mileage, Condition
Inventory A strong entity; attributes: Inventory_ID (Primary Key), VIN (Foreign Key), Stock_Number, Location
Customer A strong entity; attributes: Customer_ID (Primary Key), First_Name, Last_Name, Phone_Number, Email_Address, Address
Salesperson A strong entity; attributes: Salesperson_ID (Primary Key), First_Name, Last_Name, Employee_Number, Phone_Number, Email_Address
Sale A strong entity; attributes: Sale_ID (Primary Key), Customer_ID (Foreign Key), Salesperson_ID (Foreign Key), Sale_Date, Sale_Price, Payment_Method
SoldVehicle A junction (associative) entity; attributes: SoldVehicle_ID (Primary Key), Sale_ID (Foreign Key), VIN (Foreign Key), Stock_Number (Foreign Key)
Service A strong entity; attributes: Service_ID (Primary Key), VIN (Foreign Key), Service_Type, Service_Date, Cost

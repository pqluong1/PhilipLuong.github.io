Sleep Quality Data Analysis


This project uses R to explore various factors influencing sleep quality and duration, using statistical techniques to better understand patterns and predictors of sleep behavior.

Associations

Introduction and Findings

I am analyzing the relationship between an individual’s quality of sleep and their sleep du ration to better understand whether the amount of time a person sleeps, whether short or long, affects the quality of their sleep. This association is discovered to be statistically sig nificant, so this implies that individuals who have longer sleep duration/those who sleep longer tend to have better overall quality of sleep.

I am also analyzing the relationship between an individual’s stress level and their sleep du ration to better understand whether the amount of time a person sleeps, whether short or long, influences their stress levels. This association is discovered to be statistically signifi cant, so this implies that individuals who have longer sleep duration/those who sleep longer tend to have less overall stress levels.

Simple Linear Regression

Part 1: Analysis of Quality of Sleep and Sleep Duration

I’m using a simple linear regression to predict an individual’s quality of sleep and their sleep duration because I want to better understand whether the amount of time a person sleeps, whether short or long, affects the quality of their sleep.

Part 2: Analysis of Quality of Sleep and Heart Rate

I’m using a simple linear regression to predict an individual’s quality of sleep and their heart rate because I want to better understand whether an individual’s heart rate, whether high or low, affects the quality of their sleep.

Categorical Predictors

Analysis of Quality of Sleep and Sleep Duration, Gender, and Occupation

I’m using a multiple regression model to predict an individual’s quality of sleep based on their sleep duration, their gender, and their occupation. I want to better understand how the amount of time a person sleeps, whether short or long, as well as knowing their gender and occupation, may affect their overall sleep quality.

I’m making this model because I want to better understand how the amount of time a person sleeps, whether short or long, as well as knowing their gender and occupation, may affect their overall sleep quality.

Logistic Regression

Analysis of an Individual Having a Sleeping Disorder Based on their Sleep Duration, Heart Rate, and Occupation

I’m using a logistic regression model to predict the probability of an individual having a sleeping disorder based on their sleep duration, heart rate, and occupation.

I’m making this model because I want to better understand how factors such as sleep duration, heart rate, and occupation may influence the probability of them having a sleeping disorder.
